Jack's Page



Albums:

In order of release.

Dark Times

Released in 2020, Dark Times was originally supposed to be 55 tracks long. However, I ended up removing a lot of tracks, now being 24 tracks. It's my first album, and I'm not very proud of it. The good thing is, it kickstard my music career.





Symphony of People

Released in 2021, Symphony of People actually started being written in 2020, with 'The Fool' being the starting track. This album is definetely a lot better than Dark Times, and has my respect.





Route Point Radio

To be released in 2021, Route Point Radio is a cancelled album in the Genre of Electro Swing. It would have 15 songs, each following into the next in good transitions.





Wandering

To be released in 2021, Wandering is a cancelled album. Wandering would be a 8 part audio/music epic about a man looking for purpose in life. The album was cancelled due to lack of voice actors.





SATQTSTC 2 OST

Released in 2021, this is the original soundtrack to the now cancelled game Steven and the Quest to Save the Climate 2. The soundtrack still remains however.





Dirty Deed OST

Released in 2022, this is the Dirty Deed soundtrack. It's very short, and it's all chiptune. 10/10 soundtrack.





House Simulator 2023 OST

Released in 2020, this 1 track OST was supposed to be longer, but I ended up cancelling the game.





RecceBoon OST

Released in 2022 (and onward!), this is the RecceBoon soundtrack! With boss themes, biome themes, and all the more, this is my favorite OST I've ever written!





End of the World

Released in 2023, this Album is detecated to my late step-grandfather. Rest in peace.





Hiatus

Released in 2023, Hiatus is a album I released after a break from music. This short album does not really have a meaning other than fire tunes. :D





Outside

Released in 2023, the Outside album is just a combination of all the 'Outside' songs, which are each remixes of the original Outside from Dark Times. Every year a new Outside is released.





Colack

Released in 2023, this album is very experimental and mostly focuses on songs I want to make.





Jooz! OST

Released in 2023, this is the soundtrack for the (soon to be) hit game Jooz!. Bossfights, levels, and much more! (Batteries not included)
